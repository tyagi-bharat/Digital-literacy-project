# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## Do's
1. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Protect your accounts from unauthorized access.
2. **Review Privacy Settings:** Regularly check who can see your posts and personal information.
3. **Think Before You Post:** Consider if a post could impact your professional reputation.
4. **Fact-Check Information:** Verify news or links before sharing them with your network.
5. **Network Professionally:** Use platforms like LinkedIn to connect with industry experts.

## Don'ts
1. **Don't Share Sensitive Data:** Never post your OTPs, home address, or private IDs.
2. **Don't Engage with Trolls:** Avoid online arguments that can turn into cyberbullying.
3. **Don't Post Inappropriate Content:** Avoid photos or comments that could be seen as unprofessional.
4. **Don't Click Suspicious Links:** Beware of "free gift" or "urgent" links in DMs.
5. **Don't Overshare Your Location:** Avoid posting real-time locations while you are still there.
