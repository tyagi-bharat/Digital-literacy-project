# Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist

1. [cite_start]**UPI PIN is for Sending Only:** Remember that you never need to enter your PIN to receive money[cite: 83].
2. [cite_start]**Verify QR Codes:** Never scan a QR code sent by a stranger claiming to "send" you money[cite: 83].
3. **Secure Your Device:** Use biometric locks or strong passcodes on all payment and social media apps.
4. **Use Official Apps Only:** Download banking and social apps only from the official Play Store or App Store.
5. **Beware of Phishing:** Do not click on "Urgent" or "Reward" links sent via SMS or WhatsApp.
6. **Keep Software Updated:** Regularly update your OS and apps to patch security vulnerabilities.
7. **Monitor Bank Statements:** Regularly check your transaction history for any unauthorized activity.
8. [cite_start]**Report Immediately:** If you are a victim, report the crime at the **National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)** or call the **Helpline 1930** immediately[cite: 84].
